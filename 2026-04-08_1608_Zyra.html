Phase 1
MEDIUMDRAFT – WIN CONDITION
—

Zyra's role is zone denial from max range, not frontline support

Red team's comp has no real frontline — (A) Nasus takes time to stack, (A) Briar is a squishy dive jungler. Blue team has (E) Rakan W + (E) Nocturne R global engage + (E) Gnar R as a teamfight ult. (A) Zyra wins fights by planting seeds at the perimeter so enemies walk through CC to reach your carries — not by diving in alongside (A) Briar.

Fix: Before every fight, identify which angle (E) Rakan and (E) Nocturne are coming from and pre-plant thorns in that direction. These two always engage together — seeds in their path create instant CC on arrival.
HIGHDRAFT – THREAT RECOGNITION
—

(E) Ryze must die before 15 minutes or the game is lost

(E) Ryze ended 11/1 with 41,150 damage. He scales exponentially with mana/AP items and becomes nearly unkillable once he completes his second item. Your comp ((A) TF global + (A) Zyra CC + (A) Jhin burst) had a legitimate window to kill him at his first powerspike (pre-15 minutes). That window closed at 11:27 when he got a quadra instead.

Fix: Red team's unspoken pregame contract is: kill (E) Ryze before 15 minutes or lose. (A) TF should be ulting mid constantly from 8–14 minutes to create a (A) Zyra-snare + (A) Jhin-execute window on (E) Ryze.
