Findings

Phase 1
LOWDRAFT – WIN CONDITION
—

Lulu + Jinx is textbook; your job is to peel Jinx to 3 items and explode in late teamfights

Ally Lulu's W (Whimsy polymorph) directly counters dive assassins targeting Ally Jinx. Lulu's E (Help, Pix! shield) and R (Wild Growth knockup) are specifically designed to keep a hyperscaling carry alive through burst. Ally Jinx on 418 CS in a 42-minute game should be an unkillable teamfight monster — the pairing is correct.

Fix: No draft issue. The problem was execution.
MEDIUMDRAFT – THREAT RECOGNITION
—

Enemy Garen + Enemy Akali are the dive threats to track every fight

Enemy Garen and Enemy Akali are both hard-engage dive champions that will go past the frontline to reach Ally Jinx. Enemy Akali killed Ally Jinx twice (30:39, 41:50) and killed you at 18:24 and 38:32. Enemy Garen ended 11/2 — once fed, his Demacian Justice one-shots Ally Jinx instantly. In both Jinx deaths you contributed to, Ally Akali was the executor.

Fix: Before casting any spell in a teamfight, locate Enemy Garen and Enemy Akali on the minimap. If either is diving, immediately cast W (polymorph) on them the moment they reach Ally Jinx — not after. Polymorph breaks Akali's engage window completely.
Phase 2
11:50MEDIUMEARLY – POSITIONING
top side river

First death; both Lulu and Jinx roaming top at 11 minutes

Enemy Lucian killed you at 11:50 in the top side river (Enemy Nami assisting). At the same time, Ally Jinx was also in the top area — she got assists on Ally Kayn kills at 11:58 and 12:47. Both you and Ally Jinx had roamed from bot to top side at minute 11. For a hyperscaling carry who needs CS over kills, roaming at 11 minutes with one item delays her power spike.

Fix: Ally Jinx's scaling comes from items, not kills. At 11 minutes she should still be in bot lane farming toward her second item. Stay with her through 13–14 minutes. If she decides to roam, you follow — but ideally neither of you leaves bot lane this early.
14:48MEDIUMEARLY – RESOURCE EFFICIENCY
—

Ardent Censer completed at 14:48; target is 12–13 minutes

Your first complete item was Ardent Censer at 14:48 — correct choice for Ally Jinx, but 2 minutes slow. You had Forbidden Idol (05:30) and Aether Wisp (09:57) — the two main components — by 10 minutes, but then bought a second Aether Wisp at 12:08 and a Glowing Mote at 10:11, splitting your gold before committing.

Fix: Once you buy Forbidden Idol → Aether Wisp, go straight to completing Ardent without buying components from a different item path. Don't buy the second Aether Wisp until Ardent is done.
Phase 3
18:13–18:35HIGHMID – TEAMFIGHT
top side / top side river

The game-breaking fight; Lulu dies, Jinx dies 10 seconds later

At 18:13 in the top lane, Enemy Udyr killed Ally Darius and Enemy Garen killed Ally Kayn simultaneously — Red team lost its two frontliners in under 1 second. Rather than retreating Ally Jinx, you died to Enemy Akali at 18:24 in the top side river, and Enemy Lucian killed Ally Jinx 10 seconds later at 18:35 in the same location. Blue team took Red's bot outer turret 3 seconds after that (18:38). This 22-second window swung the gold from -181 at 15 minutes to -3,231 at 20 minutes.

Fix: When your team loses 2 players in quick succession, immediately cast W (Whimsy) on the nearest chasing enemy and walk Ally Jinx backward. Lulu's W is 2+ seconds of complete shutdown on Enemy Akali's dash — more than enough time to create distance. The critical error was staying in the fight after Darius and Kayn died instead of polymorphing the first enemy who chased you and running.
23:03HIGHMID – OBJECTIVE PRIORITY
Baron pit

Uncontested Baron; no ward, no threat

Enemy Garen walked into the Baron pit at 23:03 and Blue team killed Baron without contest or vision from Red team. At -3,231 gold deficit, Red team couldn't win a direct Baron fight — but they also had zero information it was starting. Blue team converted Baron into Red's top inner (24:37) and mid inner (27:49).

Fix: Drop a control ward in the Baron pit entrance at 22:30 regardless of whether you can contest. The ward gives your team 15–20 seconds of reaction time to threaten a pick on the exit path. A Lulu R (Wild Growth knockup) on an enemy walking out of Baron buys 2–3 seconds for your team to scatter. Being blind to Baron is never correct even when you can't fight for it.
