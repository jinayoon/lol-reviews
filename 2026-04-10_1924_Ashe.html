3 Things To Work On

1.
Complete Mortal Reminder vs Healing Champions
Executioner's Calling (750 gold) applies 40% Grievous Wounds on hit — a temporary reduction in enemy healing. Mortal Reminder (1,600 gold total) is its completion: same Grievous Wounds, plus Last Whisper's 20% armor penetration. Against Enemy Dr. Mundo, whose R heals ~20% max HP over 10 seconds, you need Mortal Reminder specifically — not the base component. The habit: if you buy Executioner's Calling in laning phase (correct situational purchase), the next recall where you have 850+ gold, you complete it before buying any other item. You bought Last Whisper at 17:22 but built it into Lord Dominik's Regards instead of Mortal Reminder. Both LDR and Mortal Reminder are correct in this matchup — buy Mortal Reminder first.
2.
Control Wards as Carry Standard Practice
As the primary carry in a dive-heavy matchup (Skarner + Dr. Mundo + Rakan), your survival depends on seeing the engage before it happens. You placed 0 control wards this game. Enemy Rakan was invisible on the minimap before the 23:35 fight that killed you. A single control ward in the bot-side river at 14–16 minutes, and another in the mid river at 20–22 minutes, creates permanent vision coverage on the two most common engage corridors. At 75 gold each, two control wards per recall is 150 gold — a cost that prevents the 300-gold death bounty plus the lost gold advantage from dying while ahead. ADC control wards are not a support responsibility. They are a carry's insurance policy.
3.
Farm Pace Vs Viktor and High-Gold ADCs
Your gold deficit vs Viktor grew from –180 at 10 minutes to –1,877 at 20 minutes — a 1,697-gold gap created in 10 minutes without a single death on your side. Viktor's AP ADC build scales steeply: each ability power item is multiplicatively more powerful than the last. Keeping pace requires hitting 8–9 CS/min (you finished at 7.2/min, or ~40 CS fewer than standard). In the 10–15 minute window, the habit is: after each recall, count the time until the next wave arrives and ensure you return to lane before missing it. Forty missed CS over 10 minutes is 600–800 gold — the exact range that closes the gap between –180 and –1,877.
