3 Things to Work On (detailed)
1. Retreating After a Kill
You died 11 seconds after killing Skarner at 3:48, and 15 seconds after killing Viktor at 27:24 — both times by staying too deep in contested terrain. After any kill in open territory, the immediate action is to retreat toward your team or your side of the river. The kill converts to a net gain only if you survive it. Both deaths negated the gold swing of the preceding kill.
2. Zero Control Wards
You placed 0 control wards in 31 minutes. In the top lane matchup vs Skarner + Dr. Mundo jungle, the river bush at the top side entry is the Mundo gank path used at 3:59. One control ward in that brush at 3:45 — the moment you pushed Skarner in — removes the information gap that caused that death. One control ward per back (~75g) is the minimum for top laners in a game with a diving jungler.
3. Managing the Gold Differential Against a Counter
Gold diff vs Skarner: +327 at 10 min → -460 at 15 → -1,015 at 20. You started close but the gap grew each interval. When you're playing into a statistical counter (56% Skarner win rate), the goal isn't to win the 1v1 — it's to minimize the deficit so Shaco's global advantages can keep you relevant. Prioritize safe CS over extended all-in trades once Skarner hits level 6.
