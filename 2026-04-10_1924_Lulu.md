## 3 Things to Work On (detailed)

**1. Recognizing the repeat gank pattern**
Deaths 1 (9:52) and 2 (11:02) followed an identical sequence: Enemy Dr. Mundo entered bot lane through the same river path, Enemy Viktor and Enemy Rakan joined, and you died. The 70-second interval between deaths means you returned to the same push depth without confirming Dr. Mundo's location first. The fix is a two-step routine every time you return to lane after a jungle gank: (1) check the minimap for the enemy jungler's icon before pushing past the river entry; (2) if you can't see them, drop a control ward in the river brush before committing to your pre-gank depth. One ward before returning eliminates 2 of your 3 deaths in this game.

**2. Item sequencing: Shurelya's before Oblivion Orb**
Buying Oblivion Orb against Dr. Mundo was correct – his R heals roughly 20% of his max HP, and Grievous Wounds applies on any hit including Lulu's Q poke. Both items belonged in this build. The mistake was the order. Buying Oblivion Orb at 17:16 first pushed Shurelya's Battlesong to 28:28 – a 3-minute window at the very end of the game. The Shurelya's active was completely unavailable for the 23:35 fight where your team lost Ashe, Ahri, and you in quick succession. That active would have given your team burst movement speed to either pursue the engage or close the gap back to Ashe when the dive landed. Corrected sequence: Ardent Censer (14:13 ✓) → Shurelya's (~20–22 min target) → Oblivion Orb (~24–26 min). Dr. Mundo's healing is still punishable with a short delay; Shurelya's impact is near-zero when the game is already in its final two minutes.

**3. Frontline positioning in teamfights**
At 23:35, you died in the blue side upper jungle while Ashe was still at the mid-side river – you were ahead of your carry when the fight began, not behind her. As Lulu, every ability in your kit serves one purpose: keeping your carry alive through an engage. That purpose requires you to be in range when the fight starts. The habit: when walking as a group, position yourself at Ashe's back-left or back-right, between her and the most likely engagement angle. When you see Enemy Rakan or Enemy Dr. Mundo make an aggressive move forward, the first action is W on them (polymorph to cancel the engage), not movement away. If you're further ahead than your carry when a fight starts, you've already lost the positioning check.
