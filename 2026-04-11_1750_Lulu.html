Phase 1: Draft
✓ LOWDRAFT – WIN_CONDITION

Lulu + Tristana: enchanter-protect-the-carry core

Lulu is one of Tristana's best support pairings: Whimsy polymorph denies diver engages, Help, Pix! provides shield and bonus damage, Wild Growth is hard anti-dive. The win condition is simple: keep Tristana alive through laning phase, buy Ardent Censer to amplify her attack speed, and peel for her in teamfights.

This was executed well. Lulu's utility directly enabled Tristana's 15-kill performance.
Phase 2: Early Game (0–14 min)
5:09bot lane near Blue outer towerHIGHEARLY – DECISION

Death at 5:09 – Ezreal + Leona caught Lulu in bot lane

Ally Lulu died to Enemy Ezreal (with Leona assist) at 5:09 in bot lane. At 5 minutes the lane was under pressure: Ezreal's Mystic Shot range (1000) allows him to poke from behind Leona's engage, meaning Lulu was taking both Leona threat and Ezreal poke simultaneously. A support death at 5 minutes gifts the enemy lane roughly 150–200g in assist gold and removes Lulu from the 5-minute drake window.

Fix: When Enemy Leona is active, Lulu should stand directly behind Tristana rather than beside her. This forces Leona to target Tristana (who can Rocket Jump) rather than Lulu (who cannot escape Zenith Blade). Use Glitterlance (Q) to poke Ezreal from max range rather than walking up.
6:24 – 6:48bot lane near Blue outer tower✓ LOWEARLY – DECISION

Kill at 6:24 and 6:48 – aggressive trading paid off

After dying at 5:09, Ally Lulu came back and killed Enemy Ezreal at 6:24 (with Tristana assist) and then Enemy Leona at 6:48 (with Tristana assist) – both within 24 seconds. Converting a support kill at level 3–4 with Summon Aery amplifying damage is a strong aggressive call when the enemy ADC is caught out of position.

Two kills in 24 seconds as a support is exactly how you respond after dying in lane – re-establish the lane rather than playing scared.
10:32base✓ LOWEARLY – RESOURCES

Ardent Censer at 10:32 – early enchanter power spike

Ally Lulu completed Ardent Censer at 10:32 for 2,200g. This is an unusually early Ardent – typically completed around 13–15 minutes – enabled by the 6:24 and 6:48 kills generating extra gold. Ardent Censer's passive (attack speed + on-hit magic damage on shields/heals) immediately improved Tristana's DPS in teamfights. This timing directly contributed to Tristana's 14:27 kill on Ezreal.

Early Ardent on a fed ADC is the correct call. This was the right item, in the right order, at the right time.
Phase 3: Mid Game (14–25 min)
5:09 – 19:36bot lane / dragon pitHIGHMID – DECISION

Three deaths to Enemy Ezreal: 5:09, 12:07, 19:36

Ally Lulu died to Enemy Ezreal three times: 5:09 (bot lane), 12:07 (bot lane), and 19:36 (dragon pit, with Xin Zhao + Ahri assist). Ezreal's Mystic Shot travels 1000 units and deals significant poke damage. All three deaths involved walking toward the objective in a corridor where Ezreal had clear aim. Lulu at 140 movement speed with no dash is extremely vulnerable to repeated Mystic Shot poke.

Fix: Against Ezreal in open terrain, move in L-shapes rather than straight lines to reduce the predictability of your position. Glitterlance (Q) out-ranges Mystic Shot – use it to zone Ezreal before walking into the open. At the dragon pit specifically, hug the side wall to reduce Ezreal's angle.
21:42baseLOWMID – RESOURCES

Shurelya's at 21:42 – roam tool arrived late

Ally Lulu completed Shurelya's Battlesong at 21:42. Shurelya's primary value is a team movement speed buff for catching or escaping – most useful in the 15–20 minute range when teams are rotating between objectives. At 21 minutes, the map was already largely Blue team's; the roam boost was used in teamfights rather than for initiation. Dream Maker was the first item (completed earlier), which is reasonable, but the Shurelya's component could have been delayed for a more aggressive support item like Redemption or an earlier Mikael's.

Fix: Minor. In future games: if Tristana is already ahead at 15 minutes, consider Redemption or Mikael's Blessing before Shurelya's. Shurelya's acceleration is most valuable when your team needs to rotate to objectives, not when you're already winning teamfights.

3 Things to Work On

#1 Positioning vs Ezreal specifically

Three deaths to Enemy Ezreal (5:09, 12:07, 19:36) all came from walking in predictable straight lines in open terrain. Ezreal's Mystic Shot travels 1000 units and his only requirement to land it is your position being predictable. Move laterally and use minion bodies as cover when Ezreal has a clear angle. In dragon pit specifically, hug the far wall to reduce his aim corridor. Glitterlance out-ranges Mystic Shot – use Q proactively to force Ezreal to back off before walking to an objective.

#2 Item completion in long games

Kindlegem sat unfinished at game end. Once you're past 28 minutes, every back should end with: sell Stealth Ward (if held), sell any remaining starter items, complete the item you have components for. Warmog's Armor from Kindlegem would have added 800 HP and regeneration – meaningful survivability for a support who died 4 times in a game where Ezreal had significant poke. Completing components is a game-long discipline.

#3 Shurelya's timing based on game state

Shurelya's arrived at 21:42 when Blue team already had map control. Its highest value is at 14–18 minutes when teams are contesting mid-game objectives and a 25% movement speed burst enables picks or disengages. If the game is ahead at 15 minutes, consider buying Redemption (15:30 power spike for sustained fight healing) or Mikael's (for cleansing key CC off Tristana) before Shurelya's. Shurelya's is a tool for catching enemies and contesting objectives – time it to when that need is highest.
