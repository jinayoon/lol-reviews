Phase 1: Draft
✓ LOWDRAFT – WIN_CONDITION

Ornn + Tristana + Lulu: late-game execute comp

Ornn + Nunu & Willump + Tristana + Lulu is a comp designed to stall the early game, hit 2–3 item spikes, then use Ornn R to start a fight that Tristana closes. Enemy comp (Sion/Xin Zhao/Ahri/Ezreal/Leona) is also teamfight-oriented with an Ezreal for sustained damage. Blue team wins extended mid-game teamfights, Red team wins if Ezreal out-pokes.

Ornn's win condition was executed correctly: survive top lane, hit level 6, provide R initiation in mid-game teamfights. This played out as designed.

First blood on Enemy Sion at 2:20

Enemy Sion was caught near their outer tower at 2:20 and killed by Ally Ornn. This happened within 4 seconds of Ally Shaco dying at dragon pit, so both teams traded a kill in the opening 3 minutes – net neutral in kills but Ornn gained first blood gold (+400g) in a lane against a scaling tank.

Early aggression in top lane translated directly into gold lead – Ornn was ahead in gold through 15 minutes as a result.

CS parity with Sion maintained despite teamfight disruptions

CS at 10 min: Ornn 87 vs Sion 92 (–5). By 15 min: Ornn 131 vs Sion 127 (+4). Despite leaving lane multiple times for mid-lane skirmishes, Ornn finished the laning phase even on CS against Sion, which is a reasonable result for a tank that was also helping with teamfights.

Solid resource efficiency – CS recovery after roams was handled well.
10:07 – 10:44top lane → mid laneHIGHEARLY – DECISION

Double death to Enemy Xin Zhao – 10:07 and 10:44

Enemy Xin Zhao killed Ally Ornn at 10:07 in top lane (with Sion assist) and again at 10:44 in mid lane (with Sion assist). Both deaths happened within a 37-second window during the same extended mid-map fight sequence. The second death came just 37 seconds after respawning – meaning Ornn walked back into the same fight that just killed him while Xin Zhao still had Ornn's gold.

Fix: After dying in a fight and respawning during the same extended skirmish, don't re-engage immediately into the same enemies unless your team has the numbers advantage. Recall first, let the fight resolve, then return.
Phase 3: Mid Game (14–25 min)
27:26 – 29:16mid laneMEDIUMMID – DECISION

Deaths to Enemy Ezreal at 27:26 and 29:16

Ally Ornn died to Enemy Ezreal at 27:26 in mid lane (with Ahri assist) and again at 29:16 in bot lane (with Xin Zhao assist). Ezreal has 1150-range Mystic Shot that punishes tanks who walk in a straight line. At 27–29 minutes Ornn was positioned in lane without allies to absorb poke before committing to an engage.

Fix: When an Ezreal is actively poking in mid lane, use Searing Charge (E) directionally to dodge the Mystic Shot angle, or wait behind minion lines until ready to engage. Two Ezreal deaths in 2 minutes suggest walking straight at him rather than approaching through brush or off-angle.
Phase 4: Late Game (25+ min)
35:30baseLOWLATE – RESOURCES

Negatron Cloak not upgraded

Ally Ornn finished the game with Negatron Cloak as a component item instead of completing it into a full MR item (Force of Nature, Abyssal Mask, Hollow Radiance, etc.). Negatron Cloak alone provides 40 MR and no active or passive. A completed MR item provides 70–80 MR plus a combat passive for roughly 800–1000g more.

Fix: Minor in a win, but on future games: if you have a Negatron Cloak sitting in your inventory past 25 minutes, prioritize completing it before buying the next component. Force of Nature is the natural completion on Ornn for its movement speed passive.

3 Things to Work On

#1 Sequential deaths in extended fights

Dying twice to Enemy Xin Zhao in 37 seconds (10:07 top, 10:44 mid) is worse than one death because the second death happens at half-HP after a short respawn timer, against an enemy who just collected shutdown gold. After dying in a 5v5, if you respawn while the fight is still active, only re-engage if your team needs your ult specifically and has the numbers advantage. Otherwise recall.

#2 Positioning vs poke ADCs in mid-late

Two deaths to Enemy Ezreal (27:26, 29:16) suggest walking toward him in open lane rather than approaching from angles. Ornn's E (Searing Charge) can be used directionally to avoid skill shots – use it for approach angles, not just as gap close into an already-engaged fight. Against Ezreal specifically, approach through jungle brush or from the side rather than straight up the lane.

#3 Build completion in long games

Negatron Cloak sat in inventory for the final 8+ minutes. When you have a core component past 25 minutes, set a mental rule: complete it on your next back. Force of Nature would have given 80 MR + 12% bonus movement speed after taking damage – meaningful durability in a 35-minute game with a fed Ezreal on the other team.
