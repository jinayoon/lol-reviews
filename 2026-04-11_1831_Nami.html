3 Things to Work On

#1 Aqua Prison timing against divers

Five deaths to Viego, all following the same pattern: Viego dashes in, Nami dies. Nami's Q (Aqua Prison) has a 0.95-second root on a 12-second cooldown at rank 1. The correct use against Viego is: the moment he activates W (Harrowed Path) or jumps from fog, Q fires at his projected landing position. This requires anticipating his dash, not reacting to contact. Practice: when Viego's W roots appear on the ground, Q the center of those roots immediately. A landed Q before melee range completely stops his chain.

#2 Positioning with minion cover against assassin-divers

In a game with a fed Viego, Nami should always position with minions between herself and Viego's jungle entrance. Viego needs a clear dash path to reach you; minions interrupt this. In mid lane specifically, stand behind the front wave rather than beside Tristana in open lane. When Viego is unseated on the minimap, hug a side wall or stay behind the wave. A support that dies to the enemy carry five times in 32 minutes is not fulfilling the protect-the-carry role regardless of vision score.

#3 Build completion: Kindlegem habit

Two consecutive games with Kindlegem unfinished (Lulu last game, Nami this game). Kindlegem costs 800g and completes into Redemption (2,600g), Warmog's Armor (3,000g), or Locket. The remaining 1,800g is almost always available in the final 5 minutes of a 32-minute game. Set a rule: if Kindlegem is in inventory past 25 minutes, the next back completes it regardless of what other components you're holding. Incomplete Kindlegem for two games suggests the build decision is being deferred rather than made.
