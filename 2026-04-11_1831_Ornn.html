3 Things to Work On

#1 Mid-game map presence after winning lane

You won top lane in both games but had 30% KP this game and 53% last game. After taking top inner tower (24:52 this game), the map priority shifts to mid-lane Ornn ult setups. Check whether your team is in a fight mid before continuing to press top lane alone. In this game, Viego was in mid for most of 15–25 minutes – that is exactly where Ornn's R has the most value.

#2 Avoiding isolated late-game positioning

Deaths at 25:37 and 28:45 were both to 4–5 man collapses while Ornn was alone. Against a fed Viego who can dash-chain through the team, never walk into open terrain without your team within 3 seconds of follow-up. Check the minimap for Viego position before crossing the river in the late game. If he's missing, assume he's waiting for an overextension.

#3 Component completion as a habit

Both games ended with Negatron Cloak unfinished (and Chain Vest this game). This is now a clear pattern across two games. The fix is simple: whenever you recall with 350–600g and a component in inventory, spend gold on completing that item before buying the next component. The passive stats from a completed item (especially Sunfire Aegis or Force of Nature) in the 20–25 minute range are worth more than saving toward the next item.
