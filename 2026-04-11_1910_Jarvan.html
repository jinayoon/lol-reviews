3 Things to Work On

#1 Control wards: three games, zero wards

The 2:15 death at mid lane and 4:07 death at Baron pit were both in areas covered by a standard mid-lane river ward. The deaths are different each game (different champions, different opponents) but the missing ward is the same. Buy one control ward per back from minute 10 onward and place it in the mid-lane river entrance before any roam. This is the one change that appears in every game analysis for this player.

#2 Disengaging after Cataclysm

Deaths at 14:29, 16:40, and 17:59 all came after fight sequences where Jarvan IV's R had been used. When Cataclysm expires, you are standing in the center of where the arena was – which is often the center of the enemy team. The correct exit is E+Q immediately when the arena wall drops, aiming toward safety rather than toward the next target. Pantheon followed up on all three deaths because Jarvan IV was still in melee range when the arena ended.

#3 Early roam ward discipline

The 4:07 death at Baron pit happened before level 4 – an early roam into uncharted territory without vision. Against a mobile mid laner with a kill (Ahri at 2:15), she has priority to match or counter any roam. Before walking toward top side in the first 5 minutes, place a ward at the mid-river exit and check Ahri's position. If she's missing, she's either recalling or following you.
