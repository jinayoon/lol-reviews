Phase 1: Draft
✓ LOWDRAFT – WIN_CONDITION

Ornn into Shen: early deficit, strong mid-late

Shen has ranged Q poke (Ki Barrier + Twilight Assault) that deals percent max HP damage and lets him trade favorably early. Ornn's Volcanic Rupture (Q) is a skill shot that Shen can sidestep. The matchup flips after level 6: Ornn's Bellows Breath is point-and-click and he becomes one of the tankiest champions in the game. Win condition: survive the early Shen harass, hit level 6, join mid-lane fights with Call of the Forge God.

The matchup was handled correctly – CS lead maintained despite gold deficit from Shen's poke damage.

Gold behind despite CS lead: Shen's poke itemizes faster

Top Lane

Gold at 10 min: Ornn 3,800 vs Shen 4,220 (–420). CS at 10 min: Ornn 87 vs Shen 75 (+12). Ornn was winning the CS battle but losing the gold race because Shen's Q poke forces healing purchases or health loss. By 15 minutes: –679 gold despite a +4 CS lead. Fix: The CS advantage is the right thing to maintain in this matchup. The gold deficit reflects Shen's itemization path, not a lane mistake.

0 – 27 minMEDIUMMID – WIN_CONDITION

Map-wide

3 Things to Work On

#1 Ornn R as a global rotation tool

Call of the Forge God has a global cast range. When Shaco initiates a fight in mid lane and you're finishing a top lane tower, R is the correct play rather than walking to join. The ult engages the fight, sets up a ram knockup, and you arrive 3 seconds later while enemies are already disrupted. Three games of low KP suggest the ult is being used locally rather than globally. Cast it toward fights you can't physically reach in time.

#2 Build completion – the component habit

Three games, same problem: Negatron Cloak sits in inventory and never becomes Force of Nature or Hollow Radiance. The fix is mechanical: when you open your shop, the first action is checking whether an existing component can be completed right now. If Negatron Cloak is in inventory and you have 700g, you buy Force of Nature before anything else. This should take 2 seconds and eliminates the habit of buying into the next item while the current one sits unfinished.

#3 Control wards – three games, zero purchases

Zero control wards across three games. In a game where Shaco is winning by 20 minutes and Baron spawns at 20:00, a control ward in the Baron brush from Ornn (who is often near top side) takes 5 seconds to place and eliminates ambush risk. Top laners who roam through the Baron pit area are ideally positioned to maintain Baron vision. Buy one on every back from minute 15 onward.
