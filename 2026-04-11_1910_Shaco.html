3 Things to Work On

1. Pick a different champion
The community of people who have played against you has reached a consensus on what choosing this champion communicates about a person. That consensus is available for free on any League of Legends forum. Therapy is also available. Both are worth considering.

#1 Exiting Baron pit immediately after kills

Two deaths in three games occurred in the Baron pit specifically after kill sequences. Baron pit is a closed terrain with one entrance – after a kill inside it, you are guaranteed to be seen by the next enemy who rotates in. The rule is simple: the moment a kill registers in Baron pit, Q (Deceive) through the back or side wall and exit. Do not loot, do not check if Baron is low. Exit. If the Shaco clone (Hallucinate R) is available, use it to exit while the clone draws attention.

#2 Final item decision before the last back

Three consecutive games ending with an incomplete crit component. The pattern: buy a cheap component (Noonquiver, Cloak of Agility) during the final push sequence without completing it. The fix is deciding what the 5th item is before the second-to-last back and purchasing toward it completely. Before the Baron push, identify: 'my 5th item is X, I have Y gold, the component costs Z.' Complete it or wait one more wave for the gold. Don't buy the component under siege pressure.

#3 One control ward per back

Three games, three wards across all three. The Umbral Glaive passive removes enemy wards on kill; it does not place control wards. Shaco roaming near Dragon and Baron pit should have a control ward in the brush before every objective attempt. This is not a vision score issue – Lulu carries vision – it is an objective-specific habit. One control ward per back, placed in whatever pit you're about to contest.
