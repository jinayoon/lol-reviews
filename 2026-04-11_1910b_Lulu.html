Shurelya's at 24:50 – 37 seconds before game ended

Base

Ally Lulu completed Shurelya's Battlesong at 24:50. The game ended at 27:27. Shurelya's active (25% team movement speed for 4 seconds) requires the team to be about to engage or disengage – at 24:50 with Blue team already at the base turrets, the active was essentially unused. Fix: In a game where Shaco and Smolder are both ahead by 20 minutes, swap Shurelya's position in the build order: buy Ardent Censer (done, 13:05), then Mikael's Blessing or Redemption as the second item for teamfight utility, and delay Shurelya's to third or skip it entirely. Shurelya's value is highest at 14–18 minutes when teams are chasing objectives; in a blowout it arrives too late.

3 Things to Work On

#1 Kindlegem completion: three games, three unfinished

Lulu game 1 ended with Kindlegem. Nami game 2 ended with Kindlegem. Lulu game 3 ended with Kindlegem. The item recurs across champion changes, which means the pattern is in the build decision-making, not the champion. Fix: when Kindlegem is purchased, immediately identify which item it completes into and track the gold required. Complete it before buying the next item's components. In a 27-minute game, this means completing Kindlegem → Locket or Redemption on the same back where you're also buying Shurelya's components.

#2 Shurelya's timing based on game state

Two consecutive games where Shurelya's arrived too late (21:42 in 32-minute game, 24:50 in 27-minute game). Shurelya's is a tempo item – its value is highest during objective contests at 14–20 minutes. In a blowout where Shaco is 15+ kills at 15 minutes, the game doesn't need tempo; it needs durability or healing for the final push. Consider Redemption (300g cheaper, provides area healing in fights) or Mikael's (cleanses key CC off Smolder) as the second item before Shurelya's.

#3 Control ward volume in shorter games

5 control wards in a 27-minute game vs 10 in each of the previous two (35-minute and 32-minute games). The rate is roughly similar (about 1 per 5 minutes) but in a faster game with Baron spawning at 20:00, those 5 wards need to be placed more intentionally near objectives rather than in river brushes. In a game where Blue team has map control by 14 minutes, place control wards in the Baron pit brush starting at 17 minutes.
