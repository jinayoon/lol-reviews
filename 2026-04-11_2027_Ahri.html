Phase 1: Draft
MEDIUMDRAFT – WIN_CONDITION
Ahri vs Azir: a difficult lane matchup
Azir's Q (Conquering Sands) can place soldiers up to 875 units away; his soldiers auto-attack for range 325 around them. Azir effectively controls a large zone around each soldier. Ahri's Orb of Deception (Q) has 750 range – inside Azir's soldier zone at most wave positions. Win condition for Ahri: all-in before Azir reaches his first soldier item (Nashor's Tooth), land Charm (E) when he overextends, use Spirit Rush (R) to dodge soldier hits in extended trades.
Fix: Against Azir, play to his power spikes: he is weaker before Nashor's Tooth is completed (typically ~15 minutes). Aggressive pre-6 trades with Charm into Fox-Fire W damage establish lane control before Azir can zone with soldiers.

3 Things to Work On (Detailed)

1. Tracking Nami+Aphelios position
5 of 7 deaths to Aphelios+Nami. These two do not appear on the minimap as a jungle roam – they move together through lane and river. The tell: if bot lane goes quiet (no pings, Nami not visible), they are roaming. In that window, position near Blue team's side of mid lane, hug the side of the wave away from Red team's jungle entrance, and hold Spirit Rush (R) ready. Nami's Tidal Wave is slow enough to dodge with a single R charge if you see it launch. The goal is to not be in a position where you need to dodge it.
2. CS vs Azir: use Q on the wave
Against Azir's soldiers, the correct approach to CS is wave control rather than individual last-hits. Orb of Deception (Q) fired through the wave clears the caster minions and leaves Ahri safe to last-hit melee minions without walking into soldier range. At level 1–5 before Spirit Rush, conserve mana for Q clears rather than poke – Azir's Q soldier poke trades favorably when Ahri is at 60% HP trying to cs. Get to 6 healthy, then look for all-in with Charm.
3. Item planning: one complete item per back
Blighting Jewel at 40:09 in a 41:59 game. This is the same pattern visible in K'Sante (Kindlegem at 39:47), Ezreal (Pickaxe at 38:52), and Lulu in previous games. The fix is universal: on every back in the last 10 minutes of the game, the first action in shop is identifying and completing an existing component, not starting a new item. If Zhonya's is done and you have 1,500g, that buys Shadowflame (Blighting Jewel + 750g more). Prioritize completing over starting.
