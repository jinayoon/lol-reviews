3 Things to Work On (Detailed)

1. Support positioning: stay behind the engage initiators
9 deaths in a 42-minute game as support puts this well above the 4–5 death budget for an enchanter/utility support. The deaths at 10:41, 11:58, 14:39, and 19:05 all came from being at or ahead of the engage tank position. As Jarvan IV support, the positioning rule is: Dr. Mundo and K'Sante go in first. Jarvan IV follows 1–2 seconds after to use E+Q and Cataclysm on trapped targets. If Dr. Mundo and K'Sante are not going in, Jarvan IV should not go in either. The support is not the engage – it is the follow-up to the engage.
2. Cataclysm timing: only when carries can follow up
Cataclysm creates an arena that traps both the target and Jarvan IV inside. It is a fight-committing ability that cannot be undone. Before using Cataclysm, check two things: (1) is the target a priority kill (fed carry, low-HP enemy)? (2) are Ezreal and Ahri within range to deal damage into the arena? If both answers are yes, Cataclysm. If no, hold it – the E+Q gapcloser alone is valuable without committing the ult. The 14:36 Azir kill shows the correct version.
3. Maintain the vision improvement
5 control wards this game after 0 in previous games is the right direction. 120 VS (2.9/min) is the support benchmark. The specific improvement to make: of the 5 control wards placed, prioritize the Baron pit brush (minutes 18–20 onward) and the Dragon pit brush (minutes 5, 10, 15). These two locations cover the objectives that decide the game. General river wards are secondary to objective-specific control vision.
