Phase 1: Draft
Win Condition — Scale and Group: Jinx's win condition in this comp is to survive laning phase, hit the Hexoptics C44 → Runaan's Hurricane spike, then shred grouped enemies with AoE rockets and on-hit. Ally Graves and Ally Naafiri provide enough early pressure to buy time. Priority: avoid solo deaths, reach the 3-item spike, then convert teamfights.
Enemy Morgana Black Shield vs Ally Nami: Morgana's Black Shield counters most of Ally Nami's engage (Q bubble / R tidal wave can be blocked). As Jinx, you cannot rely on Nami to open fights whenever Morgana has shield available. Be the aggressor when Morgana's shield is on cooldown — especially after Nami baits it with E (Tidecaller's Blessing).

3 Things to Work On (Detailed)

1. Gank Pattern Recognition — Same Champion, Same Spot

Three deaths to Enemy Morgana in the same 500-unit zone between 06:06 and 13:26. After any gank death, apply this checklist on your next back:

  • Buy a control ward (75g) — place it in the bot-side river bush or tri-bush before returning to lane.
  • Identify the ganker's preferred path — Morgana repeatedly approached from the same river angle; a ward reveals this before she arrives.
  • Play from turret range for 2 waves — this removes her gank window entirely, forcing her to either abandon the rotation or waste time under your turret.
  • When mid lane goes missing, immediately ping and back off. Jinx has no gap-closer or escape — positioning must account for the worst-case arrival time of a roaming mid laner.
2. Control Ward Discipline — Every Back, Every Game

0 control wards placed in 28 ranked minutes. Standard practice:

  • At every shop visit, check your inventory. If you have an open slot and 75g, buy a control ward.
  • Placement priority for ADC: bot-side river bush (your lane's gank corridor), dragon pit area, baron area during mid/late.
  • 4–5 control wards per game = ~375g total — trivial in a 14,905g game. The four bot-lane deaths this game cost far more than that in lost CS and failed kills.
  • Habit drill: Before pressing Recall, type /chat to remind yourself: "buying control ward this back." Make it automatic.
3. Post-Kill Positioning — Step Back, Not Forward

Deaths at 16:21 and 20:05 came immediately after kills — abilities on cooldown, Jinx exposed in open terrain. Jinx's damage requires being alive for extended fights:

  • After securing a kill, immediately move backward to your team's position — do not advance toward the next target.
  • Check what abilities remain on surviving enemies before re-engaging. At 20:05, Kaisa had mobility and full abilities; Jinx had just depleted hers on Rengar.
  • Position behind tanks during grouped fights — your job is to fire rockets into the backline, not to be the frontline. The 29,959 damage this game was generated from fights where you maintained distance; the 7 deaths were from fights where you didn't.
