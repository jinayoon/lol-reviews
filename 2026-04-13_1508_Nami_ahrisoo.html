3 Things to Work On (Detailed)

1. Roam Threat Tracking — Read the Mid Wave

At 06:06, 11:12, and 13:26, Ally Jinx died in the same bot-lane zone to the same rotation. As the support, tracking mid-lane state is your responsibility:

  • Before going aggressive, check if mid wave is pushing toward you (safe) or clearing (Syndra free to roam).
  • Buy a control ward for bot-side river on every back from 5 min onward — 75g is the cheapest insurance against a gank in your own lane.
  • When mid disappears, immediately ping and play against turret for at least 2 waves. If the Nami E is wasted and both summoners are up, you can safely ignore a push and back off.
2. Engage Timing vs Morgana Black Shield

Morgana's Black Shield (75s cooldown) blocked most of your Q bubble and R engage windows this game. The correct counter-pattern:

  • Use E (Tidecaller's Blessing) on Ally Jinx to force a trade and bait the shield on a non-critical hit.
  • Once the shield expires, immediately commit Q + R before the 75s reset.
  • In teamfights, watch for shield flashes on enemy backline — that's your signal that Morgana used Black Shield reactively, and Jinx + Nami should hard-engage 5–8 seconds later.
3. Post-Kill Pursuit Discipline

The 15:43 death happened 10 seconds after killing Enemy Morgana at 15:33. After securing a kill in a skirmish:

  • Stop and look at what spells remain on surviving enemies before re-engaging. Enemy Syndra still had her full burst.
  • Step behind your tanks or under turret for 10–15 seconds to reset positioning.
  • This also applies to minion waves — trading your life for one more kill when the wave can push for you is rarely correct. The 29,959 damage your ADC dealt in this game was possible partly because Jinx lived long enough to fire Rockets repeatedly.
