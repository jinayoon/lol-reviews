Phase 1: Draft
[— | champion select]DRAFT — WIN CONDITIONMEDIUM
Red dives early AND scales late — Blue had to win bot to matter
Red ran Briar (dive jungler), Nautilus (hook engage), Zed (assassin), AurelionSol (scaling mage), and Samira (hypercarry ADC) — a comp that snowballs dives into late-game dominance. Braum + MissFortune is the correct counter-pick: Braum's Unbreakable (E) blocks most of Samira's projectile damage and Concussive Blows can interrupt her combo rotation. But this lane edge only holds if blue avoids early deaths that hand Samira snowball gold.
In draft, identify Samira as the primary threat to shut down. Pre-game plan: ward the red-side river bush at 1:30 to deny Briar's early gank, and commit to using Braum's E as the reactive tool every time Samira initiates.

The 3 Swing Moments

20:59 — Blue takes Baron, converts 3 towers in 2:30

Ally Jax securing Baron at 20:59 was the game's biggest positive swing. Blue destroyed Red's mid outer (22:30), top inner (23:01), and mid inner (23:04) in under 3 minutes. Braum's 62% KP and warding during this window directly enabled the push window. This was the correct macro play off a Baron — the issue was Red's AurelionSol and Samira scaling fast enough to neutralize the advantage.

14:17 + 14:44 — Red takes first dragon AND Blue's bot tower in 27 seconds

The compound loss of first dragon (14:17) and Blue's bot outer tower (14:44) in under 30 seconds was the early-game turning point. It gave Red both elemental advantage and bot-side map control simultaneously. The two bot-lane deaths at 03:24 and 06:26 directly set up this outcome by denying Blue the gold and lane priority to contest either objective.

09:29–09:39 — Blue wins 3-for-1 fight despite Braum dying

In 10 seconds, Blue's Lux + MissFortune + Jax killed Briar, Nautilus, and Samira while only losing Braum to Zed — a 3-for-1 exchange. Braum's pre-death assists on all three kills was correct peeling play in a 4v1 collapse. This fight briefly stabilized the lane and prevented the early deficit from becoming unrecoverable.

3 Things to Work On (Detailed)

1. Unbreakable (E) as your primary Samira counter — every teamfight

Samira killed Braum 6 of 9 deaths. Braum's Unbreakable blocks Samira's Q (Flair) and basic-attack projectiles, which make up the majority of her combo damage outside of R. The habit to build:

  • When Samira dashes (Wild Rush) → immediately face toward her and activate E
  • During Samira's R (Inferno Trigger) → position between Samira and MissFortune with E active
  • In lane → use Unbreakable to absorb Nautilus's anchor chain so MissFortune can stay in position

Practice this as a reflexive response: Samira moves toward carry → E faces her immediately.

2. Reset lane position after a bot-lane support death

Deaths at 03:24 and 06:26 both occurred deep in red-side bot territory within 3 minutes of each other. After the 03:24 death, the correct response: 1. Walk the wave back toward Blue's outer tower or river line 2. Ward the red-side river bush before re-extending past midpoint 3. Do not contest past the river line for 2–3 minutes

Returning to the same overextended position invites Briar dives because the enemy knows you have no vision and are playing from a deficit. Giving up farm priority temporarily is always better than feeding Samira a second death bounty.

3. Complete the third full item by 25 minutes

Braum ended with Kindlegem + Cloth Armor as components — Frozen Heart was one purchase away. Two clear windows existed to complete it:

  • After the 4-for-1 fight at 18:25–18:48 (long Red respawn timers)
  • After the Baron push at 20:59–23:04 (three towers fell, 30+ second Red respawn)

Frozen Heart's 20% attack-speed reduction aura directly counters Samira's R (maximum attack-speed fire). Completing it before the 28:28 Baron fight would have changed the durability outcome of that engage.
