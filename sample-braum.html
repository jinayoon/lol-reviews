3 Things to Work On

1. Post-Kill Positioning

Deaths at 6:49, 13:33, 17:28, and 35:45 all followed winning exchanges. The pattern: your team wins a skirmish, pressure feels safe, you linger, then the enemy jungler or support arrives. After every kill, physically move toward safety — tower, warded area, lane brush — for 10–15 seconds. This habit alone eliminates 3–4 of your 8 deaths.

2. Item Timing — Build Earlier for Your Carry

Bandlepipes ✓ 11:32
→
Knight's Vow ✗ 25:34 (target: 16–18 min)

Lucian was your primary carry from minute one. Knight's Vow (12% damage redirect + bonus resistances) wasn't completed until 25:34 — after the 13:33 catch on Lucian, the 17:24 dragon pit death, and the 22:39 solo kill on him. You had Bandlepipes by 11:32 — Knight's Vow should have followed at 16–18 minutes. Earlier active protection directly prevents the carry deaths that let the enemy team back into the game.

3. Vision Before Objectives

Your deaths at 17:28 (dragon) and 25:04 (Baron) followed the same pattern: walking into a contested objective area with Blitzcrank unaccounted for and no control ward in the pit. Before your team commits to dragon or Baron: drop a control ward inside the pit and check both river entrances. If Blitzcrank isn't on the minimap, call your team to wait 10 seconds. One ward vs. a failed objective fight isn't close.
